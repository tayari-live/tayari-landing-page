WHY JOIN?

Get your kids fluent in Swahili, through fun and games.

Delicious meals

Not only will kids learn the culture, they will eat delicious freshly made Swahili meals, and learn what to call them too!

Fun programmes

Our motto is: "Why not have fun while learning." Just wait and see how much fun your kids will have!

Great teachers

Professionally trained teachers with years of experience. We teach using TPR and engage kids on their level.

  • 08:00 - 09:00 AM - Breakfast
  • 09:00 - 09:30 AM - Indoor Icebreaker
  • 09:30 - 10:30 AM - Language class (45min) & Activity (15min)
  • 10:30 - 11:00 AM - Morning Snack
  • 11:00 - 12:30 PM - Outdoor Language class (45min) & Activity (45min)
  • 12:30 - 01:30 PM - Lunch
